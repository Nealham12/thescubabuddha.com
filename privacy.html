Breathing Gas Services

Air, nitrox, deco gas, and trimix fills.

Fill services are provided by arrangement with attention to cylinder eligibility, gas-analysis procedures, and diver certification requirements.

Available fill types

Air

Standard scuba air fills for eligible cylinders.

Enriched Air Nitrox

Nitrox fills for appropriately marked and eligible cylinders. Diver verification and analysis procedures apply.

Decompression Gas

Higher-oxygen decompression mixtures for qualified technical divers and eligible oxygen-clean cylinders.

Trimix

Custom helium-based mixtures by arrangement for properly certified divers.

Before requesting a fill

  • Confirm hydrostatic and visual inspection dates
  • Confirm oxygen-clean status where required
  • Bring applicable certification documentation
  • Allow time for custom blending and analysis

Request a fill

Provide cylinder size, working pressure, current pressure, requested mix, needed-by date, and your certification level.
