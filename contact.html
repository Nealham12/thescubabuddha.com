Adaptive Diving

Access, ability, and confidence underwater.

Adaptive diving programs focus on the individual diver, practical solutions, capable buddy support, and a safe pathway into the underwater world.

Scubility instruction

The Scuba Buddha offers adaptive-diving support through Scubility-focused instruction and is affiliated with Lifewaters. Training may include individualized diver assessment, adapted skill techniques, equipment modification strategies, and appropriate buddy involvement.

Adaptive Diver Development

Training is tailored to functional ability, comfort, communication, equipment needs, and individual goals.

Scubility Buddy Certifications

Buddy training helps divers understand assistance techniques, communication, positioning, planning, and respectful support.

A team-centered approach

  • Individualized planning
  • Clear communication before, during, and after the dive
  • Respect for independence and diver choice
  • Adaptive equipment and technique evaluation
  • Buddy preparation and role clarity
Participation, certification pathway, and required support are determined through an individual assessment and the standards of the applicable training program.

Start with a conversation

Share the diver's goals, relevant limitations, swimming comfort, medical clearance status, and prior experience.
